Our Areas Of Expertise

  1. Dermatology Department – The Dermatology Department diagnoses and treats skin, hair, and nail problems.
  2. Cardiology Department – Heart-related diagnosis, ECG, ECHO, and treatment.
  3. Pediatrics Department – Specialized care for infants, children, and teens.
  4. Gynecology Department – The Gynecology Department specializes in women's reproductive health, including pregnancy, childbirth, and disorders of the female reproductive system.
  5. Orthopedics Department – Treatment of bones, joints, and fractures.
  6. Neurology Department – Diagnoses and treats disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.
  7. ENT Department – Treats conditions related to the ear, nose, throat, and related structures of the head and neck.
  8. Pulmonology Department – Focuses on diseases and conditions of the lungs and respiratory system.
  9. Gastroenterology Department – The Gastroenterology Department diagnoses and treats disorders of the digestive system, including the stomach, intestines, liver, and pancreas.
